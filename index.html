Yoga, Neuroscience, and Creativity

With Kelly Robinson

About Me

I am obsessed with science, in love with creating art, and passionate about yoga.

I believe yoga and meditation have the ability to heal, strengthen, and balance. I combine my expertise in neuroscience, experiences as a competitive athelete, and love for creative expression in my teaching.

Where do I begin? I'm a yoga teacher based out of Chicago, IL. I grew up as a competitive swimmer and was introduced to yoga while competiting for the Harvard swim team. Since that first class, I've become a dedicated yoga practitioner and received by 200 hour Yoga Alliance license through Boston's Karma Yoga Studio. My classes are vinyasa-flow based and I have taught a variety of students, ranging from very beginner to the fitness-lover.

In college, I studied neuroscience and East Asian philosophy. These two unique perspectives allowed me to develop a deeper understanding in my yoga practice. I researched under Dr. Sara Lazar, which allowed me the opportunity to study mindfulness meditation's effects on the brain.
